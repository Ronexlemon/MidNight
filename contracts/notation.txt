Notation:
  The syntax of compact program is given by an EBNF grammar. The notation conventions in the grammar:
        . Terminals are in bold monospaced font
        . Non-terminals are in emphasized font 
        . Alternation is indicated by a vertical bar (|)
        . Repetition of zero or more items is indicated by enclosing them in curly braces({})
        . Optional items are indicated by enclosing them in square brackets ([])

Scopes:
   The primary local scopes are defined in:
       1. Structure types:
                 . All generic parameter in a structure type declaration are visible only within its declaration
       2. Modules:
                 . All identifiers defined inside a module are visible only within it unless the are explicitly exported
       3. Circuits:
                 . The Circuits parameters,generic parameter and any const bindings defined inside its body are local to that Circuit
       4.Blocks:
                .  All idnetifiers defined by a const binding inside a block are visible only within it.
       5.For Loops:
                . All identifiers introduced in a for loop are visible only inside that for loop
       6. Anonymous circuits:
                . Parameters of an anonymous circuits are visible only inside that for loop.
       7. Constructor: 
                . The constructor's parameter and any const bindings defined inside its body are visible only within the constructor
